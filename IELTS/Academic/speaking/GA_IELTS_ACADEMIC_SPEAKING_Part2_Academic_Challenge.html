Academic Challenge

üéØ Part 2: Academic Long Turn Practice

Practice describing significant academic challenges with sophisticated vocabulary and analytical depth. Develop your ability to articulate difficulties, analyze problem-solving strategies, and reflect on personal growth through academic adversity in formal educational contexts.

üìã Instructions

Academic Challenge Monologue Guidelines:

  • Preparation Time: 1 minute to organize your academic challenge narrative
  • Speaking Time: 2 minutes of continuous academic discourse
  • Academic Focus: Use sophisticated vocabulary for describing difficulties and solutions
  • Structure: Clear problem presentation, analysis, resolution, and reflection
  • Evidence: Include specific examples and measurable outcomes
  • Analysis: Demonstrate critical thinking about problem-solving strategies

üéØ Cue Card

Describe a difficult academic project you completed

You should say:

  • What the academic project involved
  • What made this project particularly challenging
  • How you approached and overcame the difficulties
  • And explain what you learned from completing this challenging project
You have 1 minute to prepare and 2 minutes to speak.
