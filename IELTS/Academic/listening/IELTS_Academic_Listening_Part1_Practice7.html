IELTS Academic Listening - Part 1 (Practice 7)

Practice Session - Job Interview Preparation

This is a practice session for IELTS Academic Listening Part 1. You will hear a detailed conversation between Alex and a career counselor about preparing for a job interview. The conversation includes realistic dialogue with natural pauses and explanations. Listen carefully and complete the consultation form with short, specific answers. You have 6 minutes total - the conversation lasts about 5 minutes, giving you time to complete your answers.

Practice Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
