Practice Session - University Course Registration

This is a practice session for IELTS Academic Listening Part 1. You will hear a detailed conversation between Michael and a university advisor about registering for courses. The conversation includes realistic dialogue with natural pauses and explanations. Listen carefully and complete the registration form with short, specific answers. You have 6 minutes total - the conversation lasts about 5 minutes, giving you time to complete your answers.

Practice Instructions

Important Instructions:
