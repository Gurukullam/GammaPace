IELTS Academic Listening - Part 1 (Practice 1)

Practice Session - Hotel Booking

This is a practice session for IELTS Academic Listening Part 1. You will hear a detailed conversation between David and a hotel receptionist about booking a room at Riverside Hotel. The conversation includes realistic dialogue with natural pauses and explanations. Listen carefully and complete the booking form with short, specific answers. You have 6 minutes total - the conversation lasts about 5 minutes, giving you time to complete your answers.

Practice Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • You will hear a conversation between a customer and a hotel receptionist
  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
  • Write ONE WORD AND/OR A NUMBER for each answer
  • You have 10 questions to answer
  • After completing all answers, click "SUBMIT" to see your results
