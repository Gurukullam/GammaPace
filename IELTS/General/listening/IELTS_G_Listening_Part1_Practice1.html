IELTS General Listening - Part 1

Practice Test - Hotel Booking Conversation

This is a practice test for IELTS General Listening Part 1. You will hear a detailed conversation between Sarah and Emma (hotel receptionist) about making a business trip reservation. The conversation includes realistic dialogue with natural pauses and explanations. Listen carefully and complete the booking form with short, specific answers. You have 7 minutes total - the conversation lasts about 4 minutes, giving you time to complete your answers.

Test Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • You will hear a conversation between a hotel guest and a receptionist
  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
  • Write NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS for each answer
  • You have 10 questions to answer
  • After completing all answers, click "Submit Test" to see your results
