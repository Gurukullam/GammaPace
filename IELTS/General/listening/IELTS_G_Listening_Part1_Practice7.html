IELTS General Listening - Part 1 (Practice 7)

Practice Test - University Course Enrollment

This is a practice test for IELTS General Listening Part 1. You will hear a conversation between James and a university admissions officer about enrolling in a Master's degree programme at Cambridge University. The conversation features authentic British accents and covers course details, requirements, and enrollment procedures. Listen carefully and complete the enrollment form with short, specific answers. You have 6 minutes total - the conversation lasts 4 minutes.

Test Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • You will hear a conversation between a prospective student and a university admissions officer
  • Both speakers have authentic British accents
  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
  • Write ONE WORD AND/OR A NUMBER for each answer
  • You have 10 questions to answer
  • After completing all answers, click "Submit Test" to see your results
