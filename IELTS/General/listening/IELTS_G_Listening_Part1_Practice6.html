IELTS General Listening - Part 1 (Practice 6)

Practice Test - Car Rental Booking

This is a practice test for IELTS General Listening Part 1. You will hear a conversation between Jennifer and a car rental agent about booking a rental car for her upcoming trip. The conversation covers vehicle options, rental terms, insurance coverage, and booking details. Listen carefully and complete the rental booking form with short, specific answers. You have 5 minutes 10 seconds total to complete this practice.

Test Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • You will hear a conversation between a customer and a car rental agent
  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
  • Write ONE WORD AND/OR A NUMBER for each answer
  • You have 10 questions to answer
  • After completing all answers, click "Submit Test" to see your results
