IELTS General Listening - Part 1 (Practice 3)

Practice Test - Fitness Center Membership

This is a practice test for IELTS General Listening Part 1. You will hear a detailed conversation between David and the gym receptionist about joining FitLife Gym. The conversation includes information about membership options, facilities, and personal training programs. Listen carefully and complete the membership application form with short, specific answers. You have 5 minutes 30 seconds total - the conversation lasts about 4 minutes.

Test Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • You will hear a conversation between a prospective member and a gym receptionist
  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
  • Write ONE WORD AND/OR A NUMBER for each answer
  • You have 10 questions to answer
  • After completing all answers, click "Submit Test" to see your results
