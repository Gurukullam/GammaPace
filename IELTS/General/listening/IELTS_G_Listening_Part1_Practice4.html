IELTS General Listening - Part 1 (Practice 4)

Practice Test - Bank Account Opening

This is a practice test for IELTS General Listening Part 1. You will hear a conversation between Rachel and a bank advisor about opening a new savings account at First National Bank. The conversation covers account types, requirements, and banking services. Listen carefully and complete the account application form with short, specific answers. You have 5 minutes total to complete this practice.

Test Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • You will hear a conversation between a customer and a bank advisor
  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
  • Write NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS for each answer
  • You have 10 questions to answer
  • After completing all answers, click "Submit Test" to see your results
