IELTS General Listening - Part 1 (Practice 5)

Practice Test - Doctor's Appointment Booking

This is a practice test for IELTS General Listening Part 1. You will hear a conversation between Michael and a medical receptionist about booking a doctor's appointment at Riverside Medical Centre. The conversation covers appointment scheduling, patient information, and medical requirements. Listen carefully and complete the appointment booking form with short, specific answers. You have 6 minutes total to complete this practice.

Test Instructions

Important Instructions:

  • You will hear a conversation between a patient and a medical receptionist
  • The conversation will be played only once
  • Listen carefully and fill in the missing information in the form below
  • Write NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS AND/OR A NUMBER for each answer
  • You have 10 questions to answer
  • After completing all answers, click "Submit Test" to see your results
