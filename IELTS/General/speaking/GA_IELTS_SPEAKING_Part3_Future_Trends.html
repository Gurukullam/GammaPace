How do you think family structures will change in the future?

Tips: Discuss demographic trends, changing lifestyles, technology impacts on relationships. Consider different regions and cultural variations in family evolution.

Tips: Mention smart cities, sustainability, population growth, transportation, housing solutions, green spaces, technology integration.

Tips: Discuss online learning, AI tutors, personalized education, skills-based learning, lifelong learning trends, virtual reality.

Tips: Consider aging populations, preventive care, personalized medicine, technology in healthcare, global health challenges.

How do you think people will communicate differently in the future?

Tips: Discuss virtual reality, brain-computer interfaces, translation technology, social media evolution, digital vs physical interaction.

Tips: Consider climate change, inequality, resource scarcity, technological disruption, international cooperation needs, demographic shifts.

  • Present multiple scenarios and possibilities
  • Show awareness of uncertainty in predictions

  • Be realistic - Balance optimism with acknowledgment of challenges and limitations
