IELTS General Speaking Part 3: Education & Learning

Master educational discussions with our interactive practice studio

Part 3 Format: This is a two-way discussion between you and the examiner about abstract topics related to Part 2. You need to give detailed responses with examples and explanations. No preparation time - respond immediately with depth and analysis.

Select a Discussion Topic:

üó£Ô∏è Discussion Questions

How has education changed compared to the past?

Tips: Compare teaching methods, technology use, student-centered vs teacher-centered approaches. Give specific examples from different time periods and discuss both positive and negative changes.

What are the advantages and disadvantages of standardized testing?

Tips: Discuss fairness, measurement of ability, pressure on students and teachers. Consider different perspectives and mention alternatives like portfolio assessment.

Should universities be free for everyone?

Tips: Present balanced argument about accessibility vs funding, compare different country models, discuss impact on society and economy.

How important is formal education compared to life experience?

Tips: Discuss different types of knowledge and skills, career requirements, practical vs theoretical learning, give examples from different professions.

What subjects should be mandatory in schools?

Tips: Consider core subjects vs electives, life skills, arts vs sciences, cultural and practical considerations, age-appropriate curriculum.

How can education systems better prepare students for the modern world?

Tips: Discuss 21st-century skills, critical thinking, collaboration, technology literacy, creativity, and adaptability. Give specific reforms or innovations.
