IELTS General Speaking Part 3: Social Issues

Master discussion skills with our interactive social issues practice studio

Part 3 Format: This is a two-way discussion between you and the examiner about abstract topics related to Part 2. You need to give detailed responses with examples and explanations. No preparation time - respond immediately with depth and analysis.

üó£Ô∏è Discussion Questions

What are the biggest problems facing communities today?

Tips: Discuss multiple issues (housing, employment, environment), provide specific examples, compare different communities, suggest realistic solutions.

How can individuals contribute to solving community problems?

Tips: Give practical examples of civic engagement, discuss volunteer work, mention community organizations, talk about collective action.

Do you think governments should be primarily responsible for social issues?

Tips: Present balanced view, discuss government role vs individual responsibility, use examples from different countries, consider practical limitations.

How have social problems changed compared to the past?

Tips: Compare past and present issues, discuss new challenges (technology, globalization), mention solved problems, predict future trends.

What role does education play in addressing social issues?

Tips: Discuss education as prevention, mention awareness campaigns, talk about skill development, give concrete examples of educational programs.

How can communities become more inclusive and welcoming?

Tips: Discuss diversity and integration, mention cultural events, talk about language barriers, suggest practical inclusion strategies.
