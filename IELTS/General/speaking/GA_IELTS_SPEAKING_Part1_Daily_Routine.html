🎯 Daily Routine - Band Score Guidance

⭐ Band 6-7: Good Performance

What to include: Clear time references, basic routine descriptions, simple reasons for preferences. Use present simple tense accurately and include some time expressions like "usually", "often", "sometimes".

⭐⭐ Band 7-8: Very Good Performance

Enhanced elements: Detailed routine descriptions, varied vocabulary for time and activities, comparison between weekdays/weekends, reasons for changes in routine. Use complex time expressions and conditional sentences.

⭐⭐⭐ Band 8-9: Excellent Performance

Advanced features: Sophisticated vocabulary for describing routines, cultural comparisons, detailed explanations of routine changes, future plans for routine improvements. Demonstrate flexibility and natural expression.

📝 Key Daily Routine Vocabulary

Time expressions: "first thing in the morning", "throughout the day", "by evening", "before bedtime"
Routine verbs: "get up", "freshen up", "grab breakfast", "head to work", "wind down"
Frequency: "religiously", "without fail", "occasionally", "more often than not"
