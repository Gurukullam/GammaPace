Passage 3: Premier Housing Agency - Tenant Information
PREMIER HOUSING AGENCY - TENANT GUIDELINES

Viewing Arrangements:
All property viewings must be arranged at least 24 hours in advance. Weekend viewings are available by special request only. Tenants must provide valid photo identification before viewing any property. Group viewings are limited to maximum 4 people per session.

Application Requirements:
Successful applicants must provide: employment contract or proof of income (minimum 2.5 times monthly rent), bank statements from previous 3 months, previous landlord reference (if applicable), and character reference from employer or academic institution. International students require additional documentation including visa status proof.

Payment Terms:
First month's rent plus security deposit (equivalent to 6 weeks' rent) must be paid before move-in date. Rent is due monthly in advance by standing order. Late payment charges of £25 apply after 7-day grace period. Agency fee of 1.5 weeks' rent applies to all new tenancies.

Maintenance and Repairs:
Emergency repairs (heating, plumbing, electrical safety) available 24/7 via emergency hotline. Non-emergency maintenance requests must be submitted online and will be addressed within 72 hours during working days. Tenants are responsible for minor repairs under £50 value.
