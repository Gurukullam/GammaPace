🏊 Passage A: AquaFit Sports Center - Membership Information
Welcome to AquaFit Sports Center

Our state-of-the-art facility offers comprehensive fitness solutions for individuals and families. Located in the heart of downtown, AquaFit has been serving the community for over 15 years with top-quality equipment and professional instruction.

Membership Options:
• Individual Monthly: $65 per month (includes gym access, pool, and group classes)
• Individual Annual: $650 per year (save $130 with upfront payment)
• Family Plan: $120 per month for up to 4 members
• Student Discount: 25% off all memberships with valid student ID
• Senior Citizens (60+): 20% discount on all plans

Facilities:
Our 5,000 square meter facility includes a fully equipped gymnasium with modern cardio machines, free weights, and strength training equipment. The heated indoor pool is 25 meters long and open year-round. We also feature a sauna, steam room, and relaxation area.

Group Classes:
All memberships include unlimited access to our group fitness classes: Yoga (Monday/Wednesday/Friday 7 AM), Aqua Aerobics (Tuesday/Thursday 6 PM), Pilates (Saturday 9 AM), and Zumba (Wednesday/Friday 7 PM). Classes are led by certified instructors and suitable for all fitness levels.

Operating Hours:
Monday-Friday: 5:30 AM - 10:00 PM
Saturday-Sunday: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Holiday hours may vary - check our website for updates.
🎪 Passage B: Riverside Community Events - Monthly Calendar
Riverside Community Center - October Events

Join us for an exciting month of community activities designed to bring neighbors together and celebrate our diverse community. All events are open to the public unless otherwise specified.

Weekly Programs:
• Senior Dance Classes: Every Tuesday, 2:00-3:30 PM, Community Hall A. Free for residents over 55. Professional dance instructor Sarah Mitchell leads sessions in ballroom, line dancing, and gentle movement.

• Children's Art Workshop: Saturdays, 10:00 AM-12:00 PM, Art Studio. Ages 6-12, $15 per session including all materials. Limited to 12 children per class. Registration required by Thursday before each session.

• Community Garden Club: Every Sunday, 9:00 AM-11:00 AM, Community Garden (weather permitting). Free participation. Learn about organic gardening, composting, and seasonal planting. Tools and gloves provided.

Special October Events:
• Harvest Festival: October 15th, 2:00-6:00 PM, Main Square. Family-friendly celebration with pumpkin carving, live music, food vendors, and local craft displays. Entry fee: Adults $5, Children under 12 free.

• Halloween Costume Contest: October 31st, 6:00-8:00 PM, Community Hall B. Categories for all ages. Prizes for most creative, funniest, and scariest costumes. Registration at the door, $3 entry fee.

Registration Information:
Most activities require advance registration. Contact the Community Center at (555) 123-4567 or visit our front desk Monday-Friday, 9 AM-5 PM. Payment accepted in cash, check, or credit card.
🏞️ Passage C: Willowbrook Park - Facilities and Visitor Information
Willowbrook Regional Park - Your Gateway to Nature

Spanning 200 hectares of pristine woodland and meadows, Willowbrook Park offers year-round recreational opportunities for visitors of all ages. The park is open daily from sunrise to sunset, with no admission fee.

Recreation Facilities:
• Hiking Trails: Three marked trails ranging from easy (2 km) to challenging (8 km). Trail maps available at the visitor center and online. Dogs welcome on leash.

• Picnic Areas: Five designated picnic sites with tables, grills, and waste disposal. Site A accommodates up to 20 people, Sites B-D hold 12 people each, and Site E (wheelchair accessible) seats 8 people. Reservations recommended for weekends.

• Playground: Modern playground equipment suitable for children ages 2-12. Rubber surface for safety. Adjacent seating for parents and guardians.

• Sports Courts: Two tennis courts (first-come, first-served), one basketball court, and a volleyball net. Equipment rental available at the visitor center: tennis rackets $5/hour, basketballs $3/hour.

Wildlife Observation:
The park is home to over 50 species of birds, deer, rabbits, and various small mammals. Early morning and late afternoon offer the best wildlife viewing opportunities. Photography is encouraged, but visitors must maintain a respectful distance from all animals.

Visitor Services:
The visitor center is open Tuesday-Sunday, 9 AM-4 PM (closed Mondays except holidays). Facilities include restrooms, water fountains, and a small gift shop. Free parking is available near all main entrances.
