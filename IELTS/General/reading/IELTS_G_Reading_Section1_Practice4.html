Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) Program

The University of Metropolitan Studies offers a comprehensive three-year Bachelor of Business Administration program designed for working professionals. Classes are scheduled exclusively on weekends to accommodate students who maintain full-time employment during the week.

Program Structure:
The BBA program consists of 24 core subjects spread across six semesters. Students attend classes on Saturdays and Sundays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, with a one-hour lunch break. Each semester runs for 16 weeks, and there are two semesters per academic year.

Entry Requirements:
Applicants must have completed secondary education with a minimum grade average of 70%. Additionally, candidates need to demonstrate at least two years of work experience in any field. An entrance examination covering basic mathematics and English comprehension is required for all prospective students.

Course Fees:
The total program fee is $18,000, payable in six installments of $3,000 each semester. The university offers a 10% discount for students who pay the entire amount upfront. Financial assistance is available for eligible students through the government's education loan scheme.

Career Prospects:
Graduates from this program have found employment in various sectors including banking, retail management, and small business ownership. The university's career services department provides job placement assistance and maintains partnerships with over 50 local businesses.
