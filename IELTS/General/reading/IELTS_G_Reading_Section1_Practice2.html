Frequency: Every 30 minutes during off-peak hours
Journey time: 45 minutes from City Center to Airport Terminal

Route 12: University Campus
Monday to Friday: 6:30 AM to 10:00 PM

Frequency: Every 20 minutes during holidays

Passage 2: International Airport - Terminal Services Guide
GATEWAY INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT - PASSENGER SERVICES

Check-in and Departure:
Online check-in available 24 hours before departure. Baggage drop counters open 3 hours before international flights and 2 hours before domestic flights. Security screening closes 40 minutes before departure for international flights and 30 minutes for domestic flights.

Terminal Facilities:
Level 1 (Arrivals): Baggage claim, customs, car rental desks, taxi rank, bus station
Level 2 (Departures): Check-in counters, shops, restaurants, departure lounges
Level 3 (Premium Services): Business lounge, VIP services, conference rooms

Transport Connections:
Airport Express Train: Departs every 15 minutes to City Center (£12.50, 25-minute journey)
Local Bus Service: Route 25 operates to various city destinations (£3.50, 45-60 minutes)
Taxi Service: Available 24/7, fixed rate £35.00 to city center
Car Park: Short-stay £5.00 per hour, Long-stay £15.00 per day

Special Services:
Lost Property Office: Located on Level 1, open daily 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Medical Center: 24-hour emergency service on Level 2
Baby Care Rooms: Available on all levels
WiFi: Free unlimited access throughout the terminal
Passage 3: SecureTravel Insurance - Policy Terms & Coverage
SECURETRAVEL COMPREHENSIVE INSURANCE POLICY

Coverage Options:
Essential Plan: £25 per person (up to 30 days European travel)
Medical expenses up to £2 million, trip cancellation up to £3,000, baggage loss up to £1,500

Premium Plan: £55 per person (worldwide coverage up to 90 days)
Medical expenses up to £10 million, trip cancellation up to £8,000, baggage loss up to £3,000, additional coverage for extreme sports and business equipment

Important Exclusions:
Pre-existing medical conditions must be declared at time of purchase. Adventure sports coverage available for additional £15 premium. Travel to countries with government health warnings not covered under standard policy.

Claims Procedure:
All claims must be reported within 28 days of incident. Medical claims require original receipts and medical reports. Baggage claims need police report if theft occurred. Emergency assistance available 24/7 on +44-800-HELP-NOW.

Policy Conditions:
Minimum age: 18 years (under 18s must be covered by adult policy). Maximum single trip duration: 180 days. Annual multi-trip policies available with 15% discount. Pregnancy-related claims covered after 20 weeks gestation only with medical certificate.
